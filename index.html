OUR MISSION

We work with entrepreneurs and innovators to identify unmet market needs and devlop viable business models with including sources of relevant demographic, economic, social, cultural, and political data.

We serve as the long term sustainment entitiy for Space, Autonomous, Systems, and Ground Based Data to be used for Research and Commercialization. We aim to create new pathways to obtain, integrate, and disseminate unmanned systems and ground based data.
