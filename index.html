Our Mission


We serve as the long-term sustainment entitiy for the design, development, and application of Spaceflight and Autonomous Systems, and the data they produce so it can be used for Research and Commercialization opportunities.


We aim to create new pathways to obtain, integrate, anaylze, use, and disseminate data from space, airborne, and ground based remote and platform.


We work with public entities, entrepreneurs, and innovators to identify potential data driven solutions to problems or questions, perform market needs asessments, and develop viable business models by also including sources of relevant demographic, economic, social, cultural, and political data.

About Us


  • We aim to create new pathways to obtain, integrate, disseminate, and monetize satellite, unmanned systems, and ground base data.
  • We want to hear from entrepreneurs and innovators to identify unmet market needs, problems, and develop viable business models.
  • We are a central hub to provide sources of relevant demographic, economic, social, cultural, and political data.
  • We establish connections with finanical, intellectual, and social enterprises that are essential to the growth of new companies.
